# microtask

What if it were possible to instantaneously provision remote labor. If you can identify and describe
what you're trying to do, you can pay someone else to do the task.

Rather than having to describe how to get access to particular resources, they would perform the
task on your computer under your supervision. Rather than waiting for hours or days, it happens as
soon as possible. Rather than having to put the whole task in the description and only getting a
veto if it is insufficient, you can show them how to do something by example, and you can offer
advice while the task is being completed.

At the same time, unlike traditional remote desktop, the work is asynchronous. You can go on a
different tab and work on something else, or even delegate multiple tasks to different people
simultaneously.
